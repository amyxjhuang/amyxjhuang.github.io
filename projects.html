tissue issue


Tissue Issue is a 2D role playing game that encapsulates the desires for high quality sanitary tissue. Dylan Lim is a student who arrives at the UCSC COSMOS when a crime occurs in the dorms: a thief has stolen all the 4-ply toilet tissue from the restrooms. It is your duty to find out who did this… and rat them out!

  • COSMOS, Video Game Design program 2017
  • Made with Unity Engine, C# Programming
  • Sprites and all artwork designed using Adobe Photoshop
